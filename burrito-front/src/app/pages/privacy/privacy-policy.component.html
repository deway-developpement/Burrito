Privacy Policy

Project Burito - Teacher Evaluation System

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Burito. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information within the framework of evaluating educational quality at the institution.

2. GDPR Compliance

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we ensure transparency and security in the processing of educational data.

3. Data We Collect

  • Identifiers: Name, role (Student, Teacher, Administrator), and institutional IDs.
  • Quantitative Evaluations: Survey responses and ratings.
  • Qualitative Feedback: Open comments and suggestions.

4. Anonymity and Confidentiality

Honesty is paramount to us. That is why we guarantee that:

Student anonymity is strict. Teachers and administrators cannot, under any circumstances, link a specific response to a student's identity.

5. Data Usage

The collected data is used to:

  • Generate interactive dashboards for administrators.
  • Provide teachers with summaries for self-improvement.
  • Enhance the overall learning experience.

6. Data Security

We implement high-level security and encryption measures to protect sensitive information against unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

As a user (Student, Teacher, or Administrator), you have the right to access, rectify, and object to the processing of your personal data.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this policy, please contact the educational administration.
